03 — Commission Rules

How It Works

The process, payment terms, and what I won't draw.

Rules

  • Payment: A non-refundable deposit is required upfront before any work begins. Once the price is agreed, you pay the deposit to secure your slot.
  • Proof of completion: Once the piece is finished, I'll send you a watermarked preview as proof. After the remaining balance is paid in full, you receive the full-resolution, unwatermarked file.
  • Revisions: Approximately 5 free revisions per piece. Additional or major revisions beyond that will be charged based on scope (+€0.50 / €1 per revision, depends).
  • Commercial use: For any commercial purpose, credit (e.g. social media tag, link in description) is required as a minimum.
